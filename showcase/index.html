On this page you can find a few of the games developed using ProCamera2D. This is just a small sample of all games using the plugin. If you have a great game and want it to be added to this list, please get in touch.

Jenny LeClue (PS4, PC, iOS)

http://jennyleclue.com

This coming-of-age narrative follows Jenny LeClue, a young detective, living in the idyllic college town of Arthurton. Jenny takes on the case of her life when her mother is accused of murdering the beloved Dean Strausberry. She soon discovers that nothing is what it seems...

Klaus (PS4, PSVita)

http://www.klausgame.com/

Klaus is a narrative driven, 2D puzzle-platformer that provides a non linear, self-aware experience while striving towards a fluid gameplay that allows the player to control two characters as well as the environment.

Crossing Souls (PC, Mac, Linux)

http://www.crossingsoulsthegame.com/

An old-school action adventure with RPG touches. Explore, solve puzzles and fight against an army to avoid a war between worlds.

The Last Night (PS4, Xbox One, PC, Mac)

http://oddtales.net/

Experience a 2D cyberpunk open world, built with 4 unique districts each with distinct architecture, cultures and industries.

Super Red-Hot Hero (PS4, Xbox One, PC, Mac)

http://www.superredhothero.com/

Super Red-Hot Hero is a frenzied action-platformer game inspired by Super Mario Bros, Super Meat Boy and Megaman. Play as Super Red-Hot Hero, the guy whose heart was replaced by a cell of thermal energy.

Paranormal Minis (iOS)

http://www.ravenousgames.com

A group of doomsday preppers have been getting ready for the end of days! Luckily for them, they are equipped for the onslaught of all things unnatural! This fun shooter has you go after vampires, ghost, demons, aliens and other abominations!

Monster Mingle (iOS, Android)

http://www.cowlyowl.com/apps/monster-mingle

Build your own friendly monster! Choose different body parts to help explore the world and uncover each unique island. Discover fun creatures, musical scenery and hidden surprises as your monster plays in this magical kingdom.

Stomped (iOS, Android)

http://uglyrobot.co.uk/

Flick, trick, stomped, sick! Own the slopes in Stomped, carve, jump, grind, manual and become the raddest of the rad! Fast paced finger flicking anarchy. Coming 2016.

Tower Slash (iOS, Android)

http://towerslash.com/

Tower Slash is a casual action pixelart game that uses challenging swipe mechanics. In a retro style environment, you must take the challenge of climbing the Everlasting Tower.

Sir Eatsalot (PSVita)

http://www.behindthestone.de/games/

You are the corpulent but righteous knight Sir Eatsalot. Traverse fifteen different level and find the evil witch Hysterica, who poisoned the kingdom Gluttington with bitter lemonade to stop her!

Worldy Cup Game (Apple TV, iOS)

http://www.worldycupgame.com/

Awesome oldschool arcade soccer! Unlockable sceneries! Team commands! Super shots! Powers! 3 Player Local Multiplayer!
